Welcome

Welcome to Soccer Fans Club, our website about the soccer clubs large and small. This website is for all soccer supporters around the world. We give you the opportunity to add information about your favourite soccer club. It could be a top club side like Liverpool or Real Madrid, your local club side or even your pub side. What is of real interest is the history of your club, the great matches, the great players, the characters and the stories and facts not generally known, that you would like to share about your club We are especially intenrested in the great and memorable quotes from your club, quotes that reflect the essence of your club.

Liverpool Football Club

Liverpool FC play in the English premier and are of the giants of European football. Liverpool was founded in 1900 and has won 19 league titles, 6 European Cups, 7 FA Cups and 8 League Cups.

Duck and Drake Club

The Duck and Drake Pub team play in central division 2 of the pubs leage in county Abbeyvale. The Duck and Drake Pub team was founded in 1980 and to date has not won any major honours
