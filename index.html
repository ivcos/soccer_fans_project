Welcome

Welcome to Soccer Fans Club, a website for all soccer supporters around the world. We give you the opportunity to add information about your favourite soccer club. It could be Real Madrid, your local club side or even your pub side.

What is of real interest is the great players, the characters, the stories not generally known, that you would like to share about your club We are especially interested in the memorable quotes from your club, quotes that reflect the essence of your club.

Liverpool Football Club

Liverpool Football Club play in the English premier and are of the giants of European football. Liverpool was founded in 1900 and has won 19 league titles, 6 European Cups, 7 FA Cups and 8 League Cups.
