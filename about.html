Introduction

We are non for profit organisaion that is dedicated to the promotion of soccer clubs around the world.

Our Mission

We are a group of soccer fans who want to learn about soccer clubs all ovr the world. We are not associated with any soccer club and do not promote any soccer clubs. This website is for information purposes only and allows soccer fans everywhere the opportunity to share the magic of their club.

History

Soccer Fans Club was founded in September 2023 by a group of soccer fans who wanted to learn more about soccer clubs around the world. Our first significant milestone is getting this website live.

Vision for the Future

Our vision is to connect soccer fans around the world and to learn about their clubs.
